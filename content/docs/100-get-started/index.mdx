Raydiant makes it easy to build apps for digital signage and kiosks:

- **Use the tools you love**: Build apps using your favorite web frameworks.
- **Customization**: Allow users to customize your apps with a variety of inputs.
- **On-device caching:** Your apps work offline out-of-the-box.
- **APIs:** Backend APIs that allow you focus on building beautiful UIs.
